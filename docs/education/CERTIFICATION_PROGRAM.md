**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

The DotNetAgents Certification Program validates expertise in building AI agent systems with DotNetAgents. Certifications demonstrate proficiency at different levels and help advance careers in AI development.

## Certification Process

### Step 1: Preparation

1. **Review Learning Path**
   - Follow recommended learning path
   - Complete practice exercises
   - Study exam topics

2. **Practice Exams**
   - Take practice exams
   - Review weak areas
   - Time management practice

3. **Hands-On Practice**
   - Build sample projects
   - Complete exercises
   - Review code examples

### Step 2: Registration

1. **Create Account**
   - Register on certification platform
   - Verify prerequisites
   - Pay exam fee

2. **Schedule Exam**
   - Choose exam date
   - Select exam center (or online)
   - Confirm registration

### Step 3: Examination

1. **Take Exam**
   - Arrive on time
   - Follow exam rules
   - Complete all sections

2. **Submit Projects** (if applicable)
   - Complete take-home projects
   - Submit by deadline
   - Follow submission guidelines

### Step 4: Results

1. **Receive Results**
   - Results within 2-4 weeks
   - Detailed score report
   - Feedback on performance

2. **Certification**
   - Digital certificate
   - Badge for profiles
   - Listing in directory

## FAQ

### How long is certification valid?

Certifications are valid for 2 years and can be renewed through continuing education.

### Can I skip levels?

No, you must complete levels in order. Each level builds on the previous.

### Is there a practice exam?

Yes, practice exams are available for each level.

### What if I fail?

You can retake the exam. First retake is free within 30 days.

### Are there study groups?

Yes, study groups are available through the community.
